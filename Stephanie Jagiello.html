About Me

I am a seasoned Visual Designer with a broad range of design experience that comes from working with agency, startup, instructional design, and software application design teams. I've created interactive, ui, and visual design for clients and brands such as Yahoo, Facebook, Williams-Sonoma, CPSC, Electronic Arts, CloudOn, PayPal, TripIt and many more. Design means continual learning and evolution. Some of my latest interests are Google's Material Design language, animating vectors, browser-based design and collaboration tools, responsive design and fluid grid systems.
